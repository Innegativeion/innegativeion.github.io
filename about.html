About Me

I'm a game designer, animator, and digital artist. I'm always learning, with a focus on building a wide skillset aimed at making me a better creator of games. I'm experienced with a wide array of tools including Unity, Godot, Unreal 5, Autodesk Maya, and Adobe Photoshop, among others.


I've loved games and seen the potential in their power to instill creativity, inspiration, and empathy since I was very young. I've carried this passion with me all my life, which I channel into my work every day. I always aim to produce with creativity, personality, and fun at the forefront.

I also love talking about games, and thinking about those relationships between design and player which create unique experiences. You can find that sort of writing on my other website,
