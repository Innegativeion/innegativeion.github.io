In this puzzle game, help the robot ETNA use her power to switch the properties of objects and enemies around. Turn foes into movable blocks, or give blocks legs to walk and wings to fly, then challenge the boss at the end.

-WASD or arrow keys to move
-Spacebar to jump
-Right click to activate transfer field
-Left click a target in your field to transfer

-"R" key to restart level
-"P" key to restart game
-"K" key to skip level
